HOW TO PLAY

Follow these simple steps to enjoy the classic Rock, Paper, Scissors game!

Step 1

Click on either Rock, Paper, or Scissors to make your choice.

Step 2

The computer will randomly select its choice.

Step 3

The results will be displayed instantly, showing who won the round.

Winning Rules
  • Rock beats Scissors.
  • Scissors beats Paper.
  • Paper beats Rock.

Enjoy the game and have fun challenging yourself or a friend. Good luck!
