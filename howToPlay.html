How to Play Rock-Paper-Scissors

1. Choose one of the three options: Rock, Paper, or Scissor by clicking on the corresponding button.
2. The computer will also make its choice randomly.
3. The winner is determined by the following rules:
 • Rock beats Scissor.
 • Paper beats Rock.
 • Scissor beats Paper.
4. If both the player and the computer choose the same option, it’s a tie.
5. Scores are displayed for both the player and the computer on the main game page.

Enjoy the game and try to beat the computer! Good luck!
