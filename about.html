About Rock-Paper-Scissors UI

Welcome to the Rock-Paper-Scissors UI, a fun and interactive platform designed to bring the classic hand game to your screen. This project is built using modern web technologies like HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and Bootstrap, providing a responsive and engaging user experience.

The game is simple yet entertaining, allowing players to challenge a computer opponent in real-time. Whether you’re looking to relive childhood memories or enjoy a quick game during your break, this platform has you covered!

This project was designed and developed by Anup Banskota as a part of a creative exploration in UI/UX design and programming. The goal is to demonstrate how simple games can be enhanced with intuitive and visually appealing interfaces.
